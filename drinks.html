The Berry Blast Smoothie

Click to Watch Recipe Video

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of mixed berries
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1/2 cup of Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup of milk and vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon of honey and ice cubes

Preparation

                
            1. Prepare the Ingredients: Wash the mixed berries and remove any stems. Peel the ripe banana and break it into chunks.

            2. Blend: Place all the ingredients in a blender in the following order: berries, banana, Greek yogurt, milk, honey or maple syrup, and vanilla extract 
            (if using). If you want a colder and thicker smoothie, add the ice cubes as well.

            3. Blend Until Smooth: Start blending at low speed and gradually increase to high. Blend for about 1-2 minutes or until the smoothie is creamy and 
            all the ingredients are well combined.

            4. Taste and Adjust: Taste the smoothie and adjust the sweetness if needed by adding more honey or maple syrup. You can also adjust consistency
            by adding more milk if it's too thick or more ice if you want it colder.

            5. Serve: Pour the Berry Blast Smoothie into glasses and garnish with some fresh berries or a slice of banana if desired. Serve immediately and enjoy!

The Irish Coffee

Click to Watch Recipe Video

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 oz Irish whiskey
  • 1 cup hot brewed coffee
  • 2 tsp brown sugar
  • 1 oz heavy cream
  • Whipped cream

Preparation

                
            1. Brew a cup of hot coffee and pour it into a heatproof glass or mug.

            2. Add 2 teaspoons of brown sugar to the coffee and stir until it's completely dissolved.
                
            3. Pour 1 1/2 ounces of Irish whiskey into the coffee and stir to combine.
                
            4. In a separate container, gently whip 1 ounce of heavy cream until it's slightly thickened but still pourable.
                
            5. Carefully float the whipped heavy cream on top of the coffee by pouring it over the back of a spoon. Optionally, you can garnish with a dollop 
            of whipped cream on top

The Mojito

Click to Watch Recipe Video

Ingredients

  • Fresh mint leaves
  • White rum
  • Lime juice
  • Simple syrup
  • Soda water

Preparation

                
            1. Start by placing 8-10 fresh mint leaves in a glass.

            2. Add 2 tablespoons of simple syrup and the juice of half a lime to the glass.

            3. Gently muddle (crush) the mint leaves with a muddler or the back of a spoon to release their flavor.

            4. Fill the glass with ice cubes.

            5. Pour in 2 ounces of white rum and top off with soda water. Stir gently and garnish with a mint sprig and a lime wedge. Enjoy your refreshing Mojito!

The Sangria

Click to Watch Recipe Video

Ingredients

  • Red Wine (750 ml bottle)
  • Oranges (2, sliced)
  • Apples (2, chopped)
  • Lemons (1-2, sliced)
  • Sweetener

Preparation

                
            1. In a large pitcher, combine the red wine and sweetener to taste. Start with 1/4 to 1/2 cup of sweetener, and adjust to your preference. You can  
            use sugar, orange liqueur, or another sweetener of your choice.

            2. Add the sliced oranges, chopped apples, and sliced lemons to the pitcher. Feel free to use other fruits like berries or peaches if you like.
                
            3. Stir the mixture gently to combine all the ingredients.
                
            4. Cover the pitcher and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, but ideally, let it chill for 4-6 hours or overnight. This allows the flavors to meld and the 
           fruit to infuse into the wine.
                
            5. Serve the Sangria in glasses filled with ice, making sure to include some fruit pieces in each glass. Enjoy!

The Cucumber Cooler

Click to Watch Recipe Video

Ingredients

  • Cucumber: 1 medium-sized cucumber
  • Fresh Mint Leaves: 8-10 leaves
  • Lemon: 1 lemon
  • Water: 2 cups
  • Sugar or Honey: 2-3 tablespoons(adjust to taste)

Preparation

                
            1. Wash and peel the cucumber, then slice it into thin rounds or chunks.

            2. In a blender, combine the cucumber slices, fresh mint leaves, the juice of one lemon, and water.
                
            3. Blend until the mixture is smooth and well combined.
                
            4. Add sugar or honey to taste, then blend again to incorporate the sweetener.
                
            5. Pour the cucumber cooler into glasses filled with ice cubes, garnish with a mint sprig or cucumber slice, and serve chilled. Enjoy your refreshing 
            cucumber cooler!

The Cosmopolitan

Click to Watch Recipe Video

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 ounces vodka
  • 1/2 ounce triple sec (orange liqueur)
  • 1/2 ounce cranberry juice
  • 1/2 ounce freshly squeezed lime juice
  • Ice cubes

Preparation

                
            1. Fill a cocktail shaker with ice cubes.

            2. Pour the vodka, triple sec, cranberry juice, and lime juice into the shaker.

            3. Shake vigorously for about 10-15 seconds until the mixture is well-chilled.

            4. Strain the mixture into a chilled martini or cocktail glass.

            5. Optionally, garnish with a twist of lime or a slice of orange.
