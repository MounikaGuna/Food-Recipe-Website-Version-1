The Mushroom Risotto Recipe

Click to Watch Recipe Video

Ingredients

  • Arborio rice - 1 cup
  • Mushrooms (sliced) - 8 ounces
  • Onion (finely chopped) - 1
  • Garlic (minced) - 2 cloves
  • Chicken or vegetable broth - 4 cups

Preparation

               
                1. Sauté the Mushrooms: In a large skillet or pan, heat some olive oil over medium heat. Add the sliced mushrooms and sauté them until they 
                become tender and browned, about 5-7 minutes.Remove the mushrooms from the pan and set them aside.

                2. Cook the Aromatics: In the same pan, add a bit more olive oil if needed, and then add the finely chopped onion and minced garlic. Sauté it
                them until the onion becomes translucent, which takes about 2-3 minutes.
                
                3. Toast the Rice: Add the Arborio rice to the pan with the onions and garlic. Stir the rice constantly for about 2 minutes until it becomes very
                slightly translucent at the edges.
                
                4. Add Broth Gradually: Begin adding the chicken or vegetable broth, one ladle at a time, to the rice mixture. Stir constantly and allow the 
                liquid to be absorbed before adding more broth. Continue this process until the rice is creamy and cooked al dente. It should take about 18
                to 20 minutes.
                
                5. Finish with Mushrooms: Once the rice is cooked, stir in the sautéed mushrooms and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes to heat them through. 
                Season with salt and pepper to taste. You can also add grated Parmesan cheese and a knob of butter for extra creaminess if desired.

The Lentil Soup Recipe

Click to Watch Recipe Video

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dried lentils
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, peeled and diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • Spices and seasonings of your choice

Preparation

               
            1. Rinse the lentils: Start by rinsing the lentils in a fine-mesh strainer under cold water until the water runs clear.

            2. Sauté aromatics: In a large soup pot or Dutch oven, heat a bit of oil over medium heat. Add chopped onions and carrots, and sauté for about 
            5 minutes until they begin to soften. Add minced garlic and sauté for another minute until fragrant.
            
            3. Add lentils and broth: Add the rinsed lentils to the pot and pour in the broth. You can also add your choice of spices and seasonings at this 
            point. Bring the mixture to a boil.
                
            4. Simmer: Reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and let the soup simmer for about 20-25 minutes, or until the lentils and vegetables are tender.
                
            5. Serve: Once the lentil soup is ready, taste it and adjust the seasoning as needed. Ladle it into bowls and serve hot. You can garnish it with 
            a drizzle of olive oil, a squeeze of lemon juice, or some fresh herbs if desired.

The Teriyaki Chicken Recipe

Click to Watch Recipe Video

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Teriyaki Sauce
  • Sugar
  • Soy Sauce
  • Garlic, ginger, and sesame seeds

Preparation

               
            1. Prepare the Sauce: In a bowl, mix together 1/2 cup soy sauce, 1/4 cup sugar, and any optional minced garlic and ginger. This is your teriyaki 
            sauce.

            2. Marinate the Chicken: Place the chicken pieces in a resealable plastic bag or a shallow dish. Pour half of the teriyaki sauce over the chicken,
            ensuring it's well-coated. Marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes, or ideally, a few hours.

            3. Cook the Chicken: Heat a pan or grill over medium-high heat. Remove the chicken from the marinade and cook it for about 5-7 minutes on each side
            or until it's cooked through and has a nice caramelized exterior. You can also bake it in the oven at 375°F (190°C) for about 25-30 minutes.

            4. Simmer the Sauce: In a saucepan, bring the remaining teriyaki sauce to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for a few minutes until it 
            thickens slightly. This will be used as a glaze.

            5. Glaze and Serve: Once the chicken is cooked, brush it with the simmered teriyaki glaze or drizzle it over the chicken. Garnish with sesame seeds 
            if desired. Serve your homemade teriyaki chicken with steamed rice and your favorite vegetables. Enjoy!

The Chicken Stir Fry Recipe

Click to Watch Recipe Video

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast or thigh meat, thinly sliced
  • Vegetables
  • Soy sauce or stir-fry sauce
  • Cooking oil
  • Garlic and ginge

Preparation

               
            1. Heat a tablespoon of cooking oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.

            2. Add minced garlic and ginger to the hot oil and stir-fry for about 30 seconds until fragrant.

            3. Add the thinly sliced chicken to the skillet and cook, stirring constantly, until it's no longer pink in the center, about 3-4 minutes.

            4. Add your choice of vegetables to the skillet and continue to stir-fry for another 3-4 minutes until they become crisp-tender.

            5. Pour in a few tablespoons of soy sauce or stir-fry sauce (adjust to taste) and stir well to coat the chicken and vegetables evenly. Cook for an 
            additional 1-2 minutes until everything is heated through.

The Spaghetti Bolognese Recipe

Click to Watch Recipe Video

Ingredients

  • Ground meat
  • Onion, Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic cloves, minced
  • Canned crushed tomatoes
  • Spaghetti pasta, Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Italian seasoning or dried basil and oregano

Preparation

               
            1. Cook the Spaghetti: Boil a large pot of salted water and cook the spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

            2. Brown the Ground Meat: In a large skillet, heat a bit of olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the ground beef or turkey and cook until it's browned 
            and crumbled, breaking it apart with a spoon as it cooks. Drain any excess fat if necessary.

            3. Sauté the Onion and Garlic: Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the skillet with the cooked meat. Cook for about 2-3 minutes until the onion 
            becomes translucent and fragrant.

            4. Add the Tomato Sauce: Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper. Reduce the heat to low, and simmer 
            the sauce for about 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until it thickens and the flavors meld together.

            5. Serve: Place a portion of cooked spaghetti on each plate, top with a generous ladle of the Bolognese sauce, and sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese 
            if desired. Serve hot and enjoy!

The Birria Tacos Recipe

Click to Watch Recipe Video

Ingredients

  • Beef or goat meat
  • Dried guajillo and ancho chilies
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tortillas

Preparation

               
            1. Prepare the Birria Marinade: Remove stems and seeds from dried chilies, then toast them in a dry skillet until fragrant.Soak the chilies in hot water until 
            soft, then blend them with onion, garlic, and spices (like cumin and oregano) to make a smooth paste.

            2. Marinate the Meat: Season the meat with salt and pepper.Coat the meat with the birria marinade, ensuring it's well-covered.Let it marinate in the refrigerator 
            for several hours or overnight.

            3. Slow Cook the Meat: Place the marinated meat in a large pot or slow cooker.Add water or broth to cover the meat.Cook on low heat for several hours until
            the meat is tender and easily shreds.

            4. Shred the Meat: Remove the cooked meat from the broth and shred it using forks or your hands.

            5. Assemble the Tacos: Heat tortillas on a griddle or skillet.Dip each tortilla in the reserved birria broth for extra flavor.Fill each tortilla with shredded 
            meat, diced onions, and cilantro.

            6. Serve with lime wedges and a bowl of the birria broth for dipping (optional).
